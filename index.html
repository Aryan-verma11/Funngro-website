Questions?

We've got answers

Just click "Get Started," create your free profile, add your skills and interests, and start browsing live projects. The whole signup takes under 5 minutes. No experience needed to begin.

Yes — completely free for teens. Creating your profile, applying to projects, and receiving earnings costs nothing. Funngro charges a small platform fee to companies, not students.

Companies sign up, verify their business, and post a project with a brief, timeline, skills needed, and reward. They review incoming applications and select the best candidate.

Payments are released directly to your bank account or UPI ID once the company marks a project complete. We use secure escrow so companies deposit funds before work starts.

There's something for every skill level. If you can write, draw, code, research, or even just test apps, there are projects for you. Many companies specifically look for beginners with fresh perspectives.
